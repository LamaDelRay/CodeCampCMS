== Description ==

With Poppy&acute;s Videos, you can embed videos from the most popular video sharing portals, as well as files in .flv, .divx, .mp3, .pdf format and office documents (.doc, ppt, É). Also, the videos and files you embed with Poppy&acute;s Videos *will* be included in your RSS feeds, so your followers won't have to visit your site to enjoy them.


Presently, Poppy&acute;s Videos supports the following video sharing portals:

Upload Poppy&acute;s videos to the plugins folder, activate it, and enjoy! It's just that simple.
